ABOUT ME

I’m a freelance artist and creative currently working with VIM Magazine and VideoNow MSU. At VIM I lead a team of 15 filmmakers in creating impactful films and video content for the publication. This Spring I had the honor in shooting the cover as well as creating a documentary to tell the story behind the cover. I also direct photoshoots, from booking the studio to guiding photographers to getting their best shots.


VideoNow MSU is a show out of MSU Telecasters. We produce music related content. I’ve hosted and produced our podcast , which revolves around music and trending topics in pop culture. Additionally we’ve done music videos and acoustic sessions with artists!


My goal is to create and learn, I hope that my art that resonates with humans at a deeper level. I’m not interested in creating visually pleasing art that only hits the surface of a person’s heart. I want to create art that is powerful and impactful.

In my spare time I’m a full-time student at Michigan State University, double majoring in Computer Science and Media & Information.
